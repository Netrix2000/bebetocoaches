HON. JANET MUSEVENI, Former Secondary HM.

Hon.Janet Kataaha Museveni born June 24, 1948 is the First Lady of Uganda since
May 1986. She is married to President Yoweri Museveni, with whom they have four
children. She is the current Minister of Education and Sports in Uganda's Cabinet.
She was appointed to this position on 6th June, 2016. She previously served as
Minister for Karamoja Affairs in the Cabinet of Uganda from 27 May 2011 until 6
June 2016. She also served as the elected Member of Parliament representing
Ruhaama County in Ntungamo District, between 2011 and 2016.

Matia Kasaija is a Ugandan politician. He is the current Minister of Finance in Uganda's
Cabinet. He was appointed to that position on 1 March 2015, replacing Maria Kiwanuka,
who was appointed to Senior Presidential Advisor for Finance.[1][2] From 27 May 2011
